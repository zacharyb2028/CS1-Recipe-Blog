Chicken Caeser Salad

Description

This extra healthy version of a chicken caesar salad is composed of organic, natural ingrediants, that will enchance your eating experience.

Ingredianrts

  • Chicken Breast
  • Romain Lettuce
  • Parmasean Chese
  • French Bread
  • Salt and Pepper
  • Greek Yogurt
  • Anchovy Paste
  • Lemon Juice
  • Garlic

Steps

  1. Chop up romaine lettuce
  2. Grill chiken for 15 minutes
  3. Bake french bread for 20 minutes
  4. Chop them up into small crutons
  5. Mix chicken with avacado oil
  6. Chop chicken up
  7. Put ancovy paste, greek yogurt, and lemon juice in bowl
  8. Mix them up
  9. Sprinkle everything into bowl
